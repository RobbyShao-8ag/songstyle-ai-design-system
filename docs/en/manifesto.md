# A SongStyle Design Manifesto for the AI Era

AI does not know when to stop. It can make design faster, but it also makes it easy to add more sections, more claims, and more visual effects after the page has already said enough.

SongStyle AI Design System is a modern translation informed by Song-dynasty aesthetics. It is not a historical reconstruction, and it is not an ancient-style decoration library.

Sparse information can feel complete when relationships are exact: the hierarchy is clear, the rhythm is deliberate, and every remaining element earns its place. Negative space is active space. It gives people time and room to form associations, allowing meaning to continue beyond what is explicitly shown.

SongStyle teaches AI to stop when expression is sufficient. It does not treat less content as automatically better. Usability, accessibility, truthfulness, and business goals remain mandatory.
