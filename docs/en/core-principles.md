# SongStyle Core Principles

These principles are adaptable across brands and industries. A design does not need to look historical or use the SongStyle reference palette to follow them.

## Less, With Order

Reducing information is not the goal; creating clear and stable relationships is.  
**Review question:** If this element is removed, does the user's understanding become worse?

## Space Creates Meaning

Negative space is active space for focus, rhythm, breathing, and association.  
**Review question:** What function does this space perform?

## Restraint Knows Its Measure

When information, color, decoration, and motion have completed their task, the design should stop.  
**Review question:** Is this addition improving meaning, or only proving that more can be added?

## Quietness Reveals Truth

Design builds value through truthful content, accurate structure, and long-term readability.  
**Review question:** Would this still feel valuable after the first visual impression?

## Warmth Makes Technology Approachable

Color, material, light, motion, and language should give technology a trustworthy human scale.  
**Review question:** Does the interface feel calm and credible without becoming vague?

## Suggestion Leaves Resonance

Design may leave room for understanding and discovery without hiding necessary information.  
**Review question:** What can be suggested rather than fully stated?
